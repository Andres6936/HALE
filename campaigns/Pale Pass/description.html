The Secret of Pale Pass
Chapter One
Begin an epic journey that will take you across a continent, battling rare beasts and finding hidden treasures. As wars wage and nations rise and fall, discover an ancient secret with the power to change the course of the known world.
This is part one of a lengthy campaign for a party of 2 to 4 level 1 characters.
New players, especially those unfamiliar with the conventions of Western Role Playing Games, may wish to first play the Tutorial.
